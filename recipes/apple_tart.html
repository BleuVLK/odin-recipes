Apple Tart

Description

A delicious apple tart to serve on every occassion

Ingredients

  • A tart shell, like a sablee crust
  • 6 big apples
  • 1 tbsp cinammon
  • 100g brown sugar
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • Apple cinammon jam
  • Crushed nuts or almonds

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 180Â°C
  2. Peel and remove the core from the apples.
  3. Cut the apples into equal-sized chunks
  4. In a bowl, quickly mix the apples and juice
  5. Add both the sugar and cinammon and continue to mix until combined
  6. Transfer the mixture to a pan, adding a small dash of water and cook for a few minutes to allow the sugar to dissolve and caramelise
  7. Transfer the mixture to the shell
  8. Mix in some apple and cinammon jam with the nuts and almonds
  9. Bake for 10 minutes to allow the flavours to fully combine.
  10. Remove from the oven and allow it cool overnight
  11. You can choose to warm it up in the oven before serving, adding some vanilla icecream and cream
